MODEL SELECTION RATIONALE
================================================================================

WINNER: XGBoost (Tuned)

WHY XGBOOST IS THE BEST MODEL:
-------------------------------

1. HIGHEST ROC AUC (94.89%)
   - ROC AUC is the most important metric for imbalanced classification
   - It measures the model's ability to distinguish between classes
   - XGBoost achieves the best discrimination between canceled and 
     non-canceled bookings

2. BALANCED PRECISION-RECALL TRADE-OFF
   - Precision: 78.84% (reasonable false positive rate)
   - Recall: 84.98% (catches most cancellations)
   - F1 Score: 81.80% (harmonic mean shows good balance)
   - This balance is crucial for business applications

3. SUPERIOR HANDLING OF COMPLEX PATTERNS
   - XGBoost excels at capturing non-linear relationships
   - Gradient boosting iteratively corrects errors
   - Handles feature interactions automatically
   - Robust to outliers and missing values

4. BUSINESS IMPACT
   - High Recall (84.98%) means we catch 85% of cancellations
   - This allows hotels to:
     * Proactively manage inventory
     * Offer rooms to waitlisted guests
     * Implement retention strategies
     * Minimize revenue loss

5. PRODUCTION READINESS
   - Fast prediction time
   - Stable performance after tuning
   - Feature importance provides interpretability
   - Well-supported library (XGBoost)

WHY OTHER MODELS WERE NOT SELECTED
================================================================================

LOGISTIC REGRESSION:
   ✗ Lowest ROC AUC (86.04%)
   ✗ Assumes linear relationships (too simplistic)
   ✗ Poor precision (64.33%) leads to many false positives
   ✓ Fast and interpretable (but accuracy matters more)

RANDOM FOREST:
   ✓ High precision (89.33%) - very few false positives
   ✗ Lower recall (76.44%) - misses 23.5% of cancellations
   ✗ Missing cancellations is costly for hotels
   ✗ Slightly lower ROC AUC (94.80%) than XGBoost
   ✓ Good alternative if false positives are very expensive

BUSINESS RECOMMENDATIONS
================================================================================

1. DEPLOY XGBOOST MODEL
   - Use the tuned XGBoost model for production predictions
   - Retrain monthly with new booking data
   - Monitor performance drift

3. INTERVENTION STRATEGIES

4. ACCEPTABLE TRADE-OFFS
   - 15% of cancellations will be missed (Recall = 85%)
